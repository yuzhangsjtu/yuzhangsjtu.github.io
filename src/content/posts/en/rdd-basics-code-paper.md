In this session of the Causal Inference reading group, we discussed the fundamental principles and code implementation of regression discontinuity designs, and focused on closely analyzing the spatial RD [paper](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jpubeco.2020.104352) by Prof. Ma Guangrong and coauthors, published in JPubE. The paper provides part of its code, and the story is fascinating as well, making it well worth a close read!
