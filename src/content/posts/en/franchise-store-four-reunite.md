The summer of 2014 — the day after the gaokao ended, I went looking for a part‑time job. With four years of accumulated “gearhead” experience from high school, I got hired by OPPO as a summer temp‑promoter. That’s how my wonderful journey with OPPO and its exclusive store began.

Actually, I didn’t start at the exclusive store — I was first placed at the shop across the street, and only later transferred there. Rumor has it that before the move, I had a little stand‑off with Sister Hao. Haha.

Getting to know Sister Zhou, Sister Hao and Brother Li was a stroke of luck, and this magical bond holds fast to this day — and will last a long, long time to come.

Tonight we had dinner together, and Brother Yanglin came too. Sister Hao’s family of four was all there: a son and a daughter — truly a picture of happiness. Same with Sister Zhou and her two sons; the darling Fan is still in the top‑track class at Anqing No.1 High School — incredible guy!

Brother Li is doing his military service in Hainan with the Navy — like Brother Yanglin, he is now a soldier!

Six years have already gone by, yet Sister Zhou and Sister Hao seem younger and younger. When I first saw Sister Zhou today, her skin looked so radiant, and Sister Hao, with her newly dyed hair in fluffy bangs, looked almost like a young girl. (How can these two be mothers of two kids already!)

We are going to be super‑awesome friends — this connection is built to last forever!
