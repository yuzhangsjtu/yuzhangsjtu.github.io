The summer of 2014 — the day after the gaokao ended, I went looking for a part-time job. Drawing on four years of "gearhead" knowledge accumulated in high school, I joined OPPO as a summer temporary promoter. And just like that, my wonderful bond with OPPO and the franchise store began.

At first I actually wasn't at the franchise store, but at the shop across from it, and only later was I transferred to the franchise store. Word has it that before the transfer, I even had a bit of a stand-off with Sister Hao. Haha.

Getting to know Sister Zhou, Sister Hao, and Brother Li was a very lucky thing, and this amazing bond has unexpectedly lasted to this day, and will go on for a very, very long time~

Tonight we had dinner together, and Brother Yanglin came too. Sister Hao's family of four was all there; she has both a son and a daughter — truly a picture of happiness. Same with Sister Zhou, who has two sons; the adorable Fan is still in the key class at Anqing No. 1 High School — really impressive!

Brother Li is serving in the military in Hainan, in the Navy. Like Brother Yanglin, he is a soldier now!

Six years have already passed, yet Sister Zhou and Sister Hao seem to grow younger and younger. Just seeing Sister Zhou today, I felt her skin looked wonderful, and as for Sister Hao, with her dyed hair and fluffy bangs, she looked like a young girl. (How on earth are these two mothers of two children!)

We will be super-amazing friends to one another, and this bond will last forever!
